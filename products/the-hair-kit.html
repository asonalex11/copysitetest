      The Hair Kit

      $45.00

      Meet Helias' all natural hair kit for beautiful hair, healthy hair. This combination of Jojoba Oil, Rosemary Oil and Eucalyptus Oil helps restore and repair weakened hair to improve the body, texture and fullness - along with many other benefits.

      *Gift box not included

      What's Inside

      Jojoba Oil

      Jojoba is rich in vitamin C, vitamin E, several B vitamins, copper and zinc - the most important vitamins and minerals for nourished hair. It's strengthening properties can help to prevent hair loss and promote hair thickness.

       Rosemary Oil 

      The high nutritional value and anti-inflammatory properties of Rosemary Oil make it an excellent ingredient for nourishing hair follicles and soothing dry or itchy scalps.

      Eucalyptus Oil

      Along with many other benefits of this award winning oil, Eucalyptus has astringent properties, which help to control oily scalp build up.

      Ways To Use The Hair Kit

      Overnight Hair Mask

      Mix 3-4 drops of Rosemary Oil and Eucalyptus Oil with 1/2 dropper of Jojoba oil, then apply onto scalp and massage for 5 minutes. Once completed, cover your hair with shower cap and let sit for 5 more minutes, then rinse and wash with shampoo as usual.

      Amount : A Few Drops

      Stimulating Scalp Treatment

      Mix 3-4 drops of Rosemary Oil and Eucalyptus Oil with 1/2 dropper of Jojoba oil, then apply onto scalp and massage for 5 minutes. Once completed, cover your hair with shower cap and let sit for 5 more minutes, then rinse and wash with shampoo as usual.

      Amount : 3-4 Drops

      Intensifying Shampoo

      Add 3-4 drops of Rosemary Oil and Eucalyptus Oil to your shampoo routine.

      Amount : 3-4 Drops
